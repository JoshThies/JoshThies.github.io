Home

Brawlhalla is a free 2D platform fighting game that supports up to 8 local or online players with full cross-play! History's greatest warriors brawl to prove who's the best in an epic test of strength and skill.

Compatability

PC

Brawlhalla is available for free on Steam

Playstation

Brawlhalla is available for free on the PS4 and PS5

Xbox

Brawlhalla is available for free on the Xbox Series X|S and the Xbox One

Nintendo

Brawlhalla is available for free on the Nintendo Switch

Mobile

Brawlhalla is available on both IOS and Android

Gameplay

Choose your Legend with a unique weapon combo and signature attack kit to fit your style!

Legends

Brawlhalla boasts over 60 unique legends each with different combat styles, 6 distinct signature attacks, and a weapon combination shared with no other!

Weapons

Weapons will drop down from the sky, pick them up to alternate between your Legend's weapons! Each weapon has 8 attacks, not including the legend's signatures

Movement

While in the air, your legend will have 2 jumps, a recovery, and dodges to weave through the sky. Be careful not to run out of movement options while over the abyss!

Dodges

Dodges are the pinaccle of Brawlhalla mastery. Use on the ground for a dash, after an attack for a chase dodge, or in the air to move in any of 9 directions!

Environment

Gamemodes

Pick from any of 3 ranked gamemodes or dozens of casual ones! These range from standard stock or time battles to the craziness of Kung Foot and Bubble Tag!

Stages

Brawlhalla has a rotating pool of tournament stages, but has over 100 maps to battle on! Journey to the all!
